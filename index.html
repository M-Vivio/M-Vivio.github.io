About Us

The Atrium Rats are a grain elevation and garment distribution company from Alaska based out of Massachusetts. We are currently in Lakeland High School's Atrium, with the goal of providing Lakeland's students with high quality merchandise. We plan to expand our business beyond simply t-shirts in the near future. We also plan on expanding our horizons to other schools sooner than later, and we will spread our grain elevation tactics with us.
